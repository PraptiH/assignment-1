The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.O aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology,promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustanability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to service for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investment in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competiveness.

Bangladesh, with lots of untapped potential, is currently seeing a revolution in its youth's enthusiasm and selfless work. Known as 'Bangladesh 2.0,' the voluntary works aim to represent resilience, community, and patriotism to make the surroundings beautiful.
